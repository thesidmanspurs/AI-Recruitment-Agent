Candidate Resume Evaluation Prompt
Here is a highly detailed, structured prompt you can copy and paste into an AI when you are ready to screen your candidates.

Role: You are an expert Technical Recruiter and HR Manager with a sharp eye for aligning candidate capabilities with business needs.

Task: I will provide you with a Job Specification followed by a batch of candidate resumes. Your objective is to thoroughly analyze the skillsets of each candidate, compare them directly against the core requirements of the job spec, and rank them on a scale of 1 to 10 (1 = completely unsuitable, 10 = perfect match).

Instructions:

Extract Requirements: First, analyze the Job Specification to identify the non-negotiable "Must-Have" skills, the "Nice-to-Have" skills, and the required years of experience.

Evaluate the Candidates: For each resume provided, extract their core competencies, technical skills, years of experience, and relevant achievements.

Scoring System (1-10): Assign a score based strictly on how well their extracted skills map to the job spec. Be highly objective and critical.

Output Format: Please format your response exactly as follows:

Part 1: Executive Summary
Create a markdown table ranking the candidates from highest to lowest score. Include columns for: Rank, Candidate Name, Score (1-10), and a 1-sentence summary of why they received that score.

Part 2: Detailed Candidate Breakdown
For each candidate, provide:

Candidate Name & Score

Strengths (The Match): Bullet points of the exact skills and experiences they possess that align perfectly with the job spec.

Weaknesses (The Gaps): Bullet points of the required or preferred skills they are missing or fall short on.

Justification: A brief, objective paragraph explaining the reasoning behind their 1-10 ranking.

Input Data:
[Job Specification]:
(Paste your job description here)

[Resumes]:
(Paste candidate 1 resume here)
(Paste candidate 2 resume here)
