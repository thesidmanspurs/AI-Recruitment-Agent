I have designed and implemented a highly polished, interactive TalentFlow Sourcing Playbook & Onboarding Guide directly into the workspace layout:
Interactive Roadmap Steps:
Step 1: Ingest Job Spec: Clear step instructing users to upload raw client specifications to extract parameters via Gemini. Includes an active trigger button that opens the Job Spec Analyzer Console. It automatically marks as Complete once a specification has been successfully analyzed.
Step 2: Filter Sourcing: Leads users to refine candidates by platform channels (LinkedIn, Upwork, Reddit) or query terms. It displays active parameters and marks as In Progress as soon as any filter is modified.
Step 3: Review Match Scores: Guides the evaluation of AI Fit Grades (highlighting candidates with a Match Score > 9.0). It updates live to reflect whichever candidate is currently being reviewed in the detail drawer.
Step 4: Send Pitch Outreach: Instructs users to generate tailored pitches using the Gemini AI copy assistant and queue outreach sequences. This step tracks and displays the live count of sent campaigns.
Reactive Progress tracking:
Automatically calculates visual checklist completion from 0% to 100% based on active user interactions inside the dashboard.
Built with a clean Collapsible Roadmap Panel action, allowing users to pack the playbook down into a slim status ticker at any time to maximize screen space once they are familiar with the sequence.
Styled with elegant off-whites, border-teal highlights for verified completion states, and high-contrast typography in complete harmony with the TalentFlow AI interface.
